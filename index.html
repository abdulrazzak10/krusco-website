About Krusco Pvt Ltd

Krusco Pvt Ltd has been at the forefront of industrial scrap management in Pakistan for over four decades. Founded in 1980 as a small scrap trading business in Karachi, we have steadily grown into a trusted name in sustainable scrap solutions. With a strong nationwide network, advanced recycling processes, and a commitment to environmental responsibility, we provide efficient and value-driven services for industries seeking reliable scrap disposal. Over the years, we’ve proudly served more than 50 industrial clients, transforming industrial waste into lasting value for both our partners and the planet.
